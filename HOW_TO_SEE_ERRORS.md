## Step 3: If You Still Can't See Console

1. Click **View** in the menu bar
2. Click **Show Debug Area** (or press `Shift + Cmd + Y`)
3. You should see panels at the bottom
4. The right panel is the **console** - that's where errors appear

- Red snackbars appear at the bottom of the screen
