## üìÖ **REVISED WEEK 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-7)**

### **Day 1: Your First Virtual Trade**
**Duration:** 20 minutes  
**Goal:** Complete first paper trade  
**Focus:** Understanding the basics safely  

#### **Lessons:**

**1. Orion Paper Trading Setup (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "You're 14-18, so we start with paper trading. This is your safe training ground."
- **Action:** Set up $10,000 virtual account in Orion
- **Safety Note:** "No real money until you're 18 and have completed this course"
- **Reward:** Unlock "First Steps" badge

**2. Your First Virtual Trade (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Buy one share of a company you know - Apple, Tesla, or Google"
- **Action:** Execute your first paper trade in Orion
- **Learning:** Understand buy/sell mechanics
- **Reward:** Unlock "Trader" badge

**3. Daily Checkpoint (5 minutes)**
- **Content:** "What did you learn? What questions do you have?"
- **Action:** Complete Orion's daily reflection
- **Retention:** Spaced repetition of key concepts
- **Reward:** XP points for completion

---

### **Day 2: Understanding Money Flow**
**Duration:** 20 minutes  
**Goal:** Learn how money moves in markets  
**Focus:** Economic basics without pressure  

#### **Lessons:**

**1. Warren Buffett's Circle of Competence (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Only invest in companies you understand. What companies do you use daily?"
- **Action:** Add 3 familiar companies to your Orion watchlist
- **Learning:** Focus on what you know
- **Reward:** Unlock "Smart Investor" badge

**2. Orion's Market Scanner (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Use Orion's tools to find stocks with high volume and movement"
- **Action:** Scan for 3 active stocks and add to watchlist
- **Learning:** Market dynamics
- **Reward:** Unlock "Market Scanner" badge

**3. Knowledge Check (5 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Quick quiz: What is a stock? What is a market?"
- **Action:** Complete Orion's knowledge check
- **Retention:** Reinforce learning
- **Reward:** XP points for correct answers

---

### **Day 3: Risk Management Basics**
**Duration:** 20 minutes  
**Goal:** Learn to protect your virtual money  
**Focus:** Safety first approach  

#### **Lessons:**

**1. The 1% Rule - Warren Buffett's Rule #1 (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Never risk more than 1% of your account on one trade. This protects you."
- **Action:** Set position size to 1% of your $10,000 virtual account ($100 per trade)
- **Learning:** Risk management is crucial
- **Reward:** Unlock "Risk Manager" badge

**2. Stop Losses in Orion (10 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Always set a stop loss. If a stock drops 5%, it automatically sells."
- **Action:** Set stop losses on all your paper trades
- **Learning:** Automated risk protection
- **Reward:** Unlock "Safety First" badge

**3. Weekly Goal Setting (5 minutes)**
- **Content:** "Set a realistic goal for this week. Focus on learning, not earning."
- **Action:** Set weekly learning goal in Orion
- **Retention:** Goal-oriented learning
- **Reward:** Unlock "Goal Setter" badge
